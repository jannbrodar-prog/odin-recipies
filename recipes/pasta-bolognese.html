Pasta bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti (long strings of pasta) with an Italian rag√π (meat sauce) made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese. Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp
  3. When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.
